Start it in a terminal:

joe-http --host 127.0.0.1

Then click the joe icon in your menu bar again. If you don't have a token yet, joe-http generates one on first launch and saves it to ~/.joe-agent/http-token.

This page only shows when the local dashboard isn't available. Once it is, every click of the menu-bar icon shows the real thing.
